Tutorial

WASD = Move

E = Interaction
(collect items, talk, enter the boat, enter houses, etc.)

Left Click = Melee attack / boat attack / fishing

Right Click = Ranged attack

Shift* = Attack block / shield
*Shield animation not implemented yet.

Space = Boat dash

B = Enter the boat interior while sailing

Panels

Esc = Main menu

I = Inventory

J = Quests

O = Boat panel

How to Save the Game

Sleep at the village camp or inside the boat.

How to Buy a Boat

Talk to Durak.
